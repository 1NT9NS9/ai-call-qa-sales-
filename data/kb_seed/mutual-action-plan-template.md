# Mutual Action Plan Template

A mutual action plan keeps a deal moving by making both sides accountable.

Core sections:

- Business goal: what outcome the customer wants and why it matters now.
- Stakeholders: champion, approver, procurement, security, and implementation owner.
- Milestones: evaluation, validation, approval, legal, and go-live dates.
- Risks: open blockers that could delay the purchase.
- Next action: the single immediate step with owner and due date.

Good action-plan habits:

- Build it together on the call instead of sending it later with no discussion.
- Tie each milestone to a real internal process.
- Update it after every significant customer interaction.
- Call out slips early instead of pretending the original date still holds.

An action plan is weak when it contains only vendor tasks. It is strong when the buyer owns visible steps and deadlines too.
